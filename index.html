Welcome to Sidoarjo!

Sidoarjo is a city next to Surabaya located in Jawa Timur, Indonesia. Known for its Lumpur Lapindo and Petis. Sidoarjo also has a name City of Shrimp

With a population of approximately 2,21 Million residents, Sidoarjo is famous for its severe traffic jam at gedangan intersection that is now being memes in social media.
